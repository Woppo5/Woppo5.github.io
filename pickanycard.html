Pick any card

Effect: Guess the Spectators cars

Performance:

fan out the deck in front of the spectator

tell them to pick any card

have them cut the deck and tell them to place there card down on either top half

peak for a quick second at the bottom card of the half they did not put there card on

put that half on top of the half there card is on

look through the deck for the card you noted and right in front of it is there card

The 4 aces

Effect: Brings all the aces to the top of each pile

Guide:

Set up: put all the aces on top of the deck before hand

Performance:

Split the deck in 4 piles Noting where the aces are
tell the spectator to take a pile without aces don't tell them that

have them take three cards from the top and place them on the bottom tell now tell them to take three more from the top and place each card on each pile

Do the past step 2 more times

Now tell them to pick up the deck with the aces and take three card from the top and put them on the bottom and place three cards from the top on each pile

say the magic words and flip all the top cards over
